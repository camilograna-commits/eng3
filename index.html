De Architecture - Liber VIII

  • Pozzolana: Volcanic ash that allows concrete to set underwater.
  • Putlogs (Mechinales): Holes left in walls to anchor cantilever scaffolding, saving timber.
  • Treadwheel Crane: Giant wheel where operators walked to lift heavy granite blocks.
  • Opus Signinum: Waterproof mortar made of lime and crushed ceramics.
  • Inverted Siphon: Lead pipes used to cross valleys using water pressure.
